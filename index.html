Our Story

Building bridges across cultures at TH OWL

From Vision to Reality

Started by a group of passionate students at TH OWL, Locali was born from a simple observation: despite having over 6,000 students from 77 countries, our campus lacked the vibrant community it deserved.

With support from the university's entrepreneurship program, we're transforming how students connect and engage on campus.
