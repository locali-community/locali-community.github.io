Your Brand, Your Platform

The power of a white-label solution for your university

What is a White-Label Solution?

Locali provides a fully customizable platform that becomes your university's own branded app. While we handle all the complex technology and maintenance, your students experience a seamless digital campus that feels 100% native to your institution.

The app is published under your university's name in app stores, with your branding, colors, and identity - while benefiting from all the features we've developed through years of campus community research.

Complete Brand Integration

Custom colors, logos, and styling to match your university's visual identity perfectly.

On-Premises Data Control

All data remains within your university systems for complete privacy compliance.

Automatic Updates

Benefit from continuous improvements without additional development costs.

What Locali Offers Your University

Easy Student Sign-In

Students authenticate seamlessly using their university email credentials through LDAP/Shibboleth/SAML integration. Automatic role assignment ensures appropriate access levels for students, faculty, and staff.

Comprehensive Admin Dashboard

University administrators gain powerful tools to monitor engagement, manage content, and analyze student participation across all events and communications.

Guest Mode: Showcase Your Campus

Prospective students can explore campus life before enrolling through our special guest mode. This powerful recruitment tool allows future students to:

  • Browse upcoming events and activities
  • Ask current students questions about campus life
  • Get a genuine feel for your university's community

Automated Cross-Posting

Save time and maximize reach with our innovative automated posting feature:

  • Post once in Locali, distribute to Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
  • Maintain consistent messaging across all platforms
  • Optional approval workflow for university communications
